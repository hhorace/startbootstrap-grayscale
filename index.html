A free, accurate, convenient, roll call system.

Get Started

Why?

Teachers usually do the roll call in class, which taking lots of time to do so.
However, the traditional method, namely calling students' name one by one, is not only time-wasting but also subject to cheat.
We need a new way to improve this situation instead of just giving up and leting students skip class.
We need to make teachers take back control, to have enjoyable teacher-student interaction again.

Features

The main idea of the RollCall is replacing the traditional way by scanning qr-code. Teachers can show a qr-code on the projector screen, and students can scan the qr-code to complete attendance. RollCall may divide to two types, teacher-side and student-side. Detail steps show as follows.

Step 1:

Teachers sign in the website with teachers' account and new a course.

Step 2:

Teachers show the qr-code at any place on the projector screen, even covering the course powerpoint, so that students can complete attendance during teachers lecturing without pausing the class.
Note that the qr-code may change every few seconds, preventing from cheating of taking a photo and sending to the absent classmates.

Step 3:

Students sign in the app on smartphone and scan the qr-code on the projector screen to complete attendance without pausing the lecture.
Note that the app would memorize the smartphone ID with student account so that students can't sign in the absent classmates' account to complete attendance.

Step 4:

Attendance of students in this course may show in a table so that teachers can check if the students are absent. There's also some graphs or charts of the attendance in this class.
